Welcome to SOFII!: a Solutions OF Interest Identifier. SOFII automate a solution selection approach of interest in a multi-criteria decision problem. This task arises in multiple scenarios such as the (a posteriori) evaluation of optimal or high quality solutions of an optimization problem. SOFII requires a very simple data input and provides as output a comprehensive evaluation of the solutions so that the decision maker can choose the most suitable ones for a particular situation. More details are provided in the Help section.
